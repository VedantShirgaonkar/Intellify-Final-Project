About Sign Language Translator

Learn more about our mission and technology

Our Mission

We are dedicated to breaking down communication barriers by providing real-time sign language translation technology. Our goal is to make communication more accessible for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

How It Works

Our system uses advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to recognize hand gestures and facial expressions in real-time, translating them into spoken or written language.

Features

  • Real-time gesture recognition
  • Multiple language support
  • Voice output with customizable voices
  • High accuracy translation
  • User-friendly interface

Technology Stack

Built with Flask, MediaPipe, TensorFlow, and modern web technologies to provide a seamless user experience.
